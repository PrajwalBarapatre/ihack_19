General Rules

Hardware Category

  • Every team participating in the Hardware Track must use/include Texas Instruments microprocessor/controller in their prototype. Every important component used must be disclosed before coming at the hackathon.
  • Prototyping facilities will be available for both students and professionals free of cost upto a certain limit. All the teams must inform beforehand the prototyping facilities which will be required for the hackathon.
  • Any team can order a fixed budget of TI Components ($60) worth free of cost. If it exceeds the amount, they are liable to pay the remaining cost. Items covered under fixed budget are marked separately.
  • E-Cell IIT Bombay reserves the right to change and cancel any point/clause/prize any time.
  • Not everyone who is registering for the hackathon will be invited to IIT Bombay. Based on the inputs provided in the registration form, selected teams would be invited.
  • All the teams must submit the list of items (including TI components worth $60 free of cost) before 19th January, 2018 if they are bringing with them at the venue for the hackathon. Any non disclosed items would not be allowed in the event. Teams however can bring their own laptops/mouse/keyboards without disclosure.
  • Each team must submit the preferred list of items in the registration form itself. If any changes are there for TI components, Teams must communicate to us at ihack@ecell.in on or before 22nd January, 2018.
  • All queries must be redirected to ihack@ecell.in with the subject "i-Hack query"
  • Teams are forbidden to build anything from before.
